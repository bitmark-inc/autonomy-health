Saving Autonomy data to your PDE

All data that you submit in the Autonomy app will be saved automatically to your personal data enclae (PDE).

The Autonomy app presently supports the following types of data to be saved to your PDE:

Resource ratings

You can view all Autonomy data that has been saved to your PDE, by exercising your right to do so.
back
Reading Autonomy data from your PDE

If you exercise your right to read your Autonomy data from your personal data enclave (PDE), Autonolmy will prepare a .zip file containing all data secured in your PDE for you to download.

Depending on how much data you’ve accumulated, the export will take a little time to process. After the export process has finished, you will be prompted to download the file.

Deleting your Autonomy data from your PDE

Deleting your data will completely erase all Autonolmy data secured in your personal data enclave (PDE).

Since there will v no way for Bitmark or anyone else to recover your data after you’ve deleted it, we highly recommend that you first export your data before deleting it.

After deleting your data, you will automatically be signed out of Autonomy.
