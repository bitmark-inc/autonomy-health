Recovery key

Your recovery key allows you to fully recover your Autonomy account even if your device is lost or damaged. It is the only way to recover your account.

Autonomy does not have access to your recovery key or store it anywhere for you.

Please make sure you are in a safe location then write down your recovery key on a piece of paper and keep it safe.
