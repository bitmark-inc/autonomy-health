Dear Berkeley resident,

Welcome to Autonomy! We are excited to officially launch the app exclusively in the Berkeley community.

If you were with us during the UC Berkeley research study - welcome back! Your input in the past few weeks has been valuable in helping us continue this project.

If you’re a new member, welcome aboard! We created Autonomy to help the community - researchers, citizens, you - become more informed about COVID-19 in Berkeley.

What can I do?

- Weekly check-ins, to help us understand how you’re doing.
- Rate essential resources, to see how “healthy” businesses near you are.
- Help us! We are receptive to all forms of feedback, and will do our best to serve you.

We hope you stick with us as we develop a valuable app for you, together.

Sincerely,

Fellow Cal Bears and the Bitmark team
