Your data

All your data on Autonomy is anonymous and non-personally-identifiable. It is automatically submitted to show the community data trends and can be used for community-level pooling and research.

Export your data

Autonomy will prepare a .zip file with your data for you to download.

The following data will be exported:

Resource ratings

Depending on how much data you have, the export might take some time to process. Once it’s done processing, you will be prompted to download the file.

Delete your data

Deleting your data will completely erase all your data in Autonomy.

Since there is no way for Bitmark or anyone else to recover your data after you’ve deleted it, we highly recommend that you first export your data.

After deleting your data, you will automatically be signed out of Autonomy.
