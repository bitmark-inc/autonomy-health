Your account

Creating an Autonomy account does not require an email address or any other personal information.

Your account gives you rights to:

Save, read, and delete Autonomy data from your personal data enclave (PDE)

No one other than you will ever be able to access your PDE or the data you've stored there.

Submit and read anonymized, non-personally-identifiable Autonomy data from the UC Berkeley community data enclave (CDE) for community-level pooling and research.

The data you share with the Berkely community can never be traced back to you.
